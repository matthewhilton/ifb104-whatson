Brisbane Art Guide is an independent digital visual arts guide; showcasing and archiving arts events taking place in and around Brisbane. The platform is an access point and resource for the online public wishing to learn more about Brisbane’s visual arts sector and vibrant arts culture.

BNE ART is an online-based artist-run initiative established in Brisbane in 2009. BNE ART supports the development of Brisbane’s creative industry by shaping new ways in which the arts are accessed, viewed and shared.
